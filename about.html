About Us

FarmAssist is a multipurpose agricultural platform. FarmAssist is a multipurpose vendor, e-commerce, educational and informative platform for Agricultural enthusiasts/experts. Users must create an account mentioning their role (agronomists|farmers|soil experts|traders) and their approval via respected authority is mandatory to secure a role in the database. Consequently, the App can be considered to be a bridging app, developed to bridge the gap between farmers and their end users, the gap between farmers and latest industry trends, the gap between farmers and current climate updates and changes, and lastly bridging the gap between farmers and important details about soil selection and grooming. It is a useful app see developed predominantly for farmers. It is the first multipurpose agricultural app/platform for farmers that assists in closing the gap between farmers and the end users of their products, closing the gap between suppliers of seeds, mechanical equipment and the likes. The application could simply be summed up as a full blown agricultural awareness platform that provides significant information about climate changes, soil types of functions, farm products and how to access such products and how farmers/traders can interact to boost the nations economy.

Unique Features.
  • Professional guidance from agronomist experts.
  • Farmers get to interact directly with users of their products.
  • Online training/sensitization.
